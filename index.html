Our Philosophy

We don't pose
moments —
we find them.

First Memory Photo is a boutique studio rooted in South India, dedicated to quiet, intentional wedding storytelling. We blend into your celebration so every frame feels naturally true.

Our approach is unhurried and unobtrusive. Working with natural light and real emotions, we believe the best photographs emerge when people feel safe enough to simply be themselves.

Based in Chennai, we travel across India and internationally to document your most important day with care and craft.

About

Rooted in
memory

"I don't just take photographs — I preserve the feelings that make a day impossible to forget."

First Memory Photo began with a simple conviction: the photographs from your wedding day will be the ones your children look at decades from now. Every love story deserves that kind of permanence.

With a quiet, unhurried presence, we work with natural light and genuine emotion — no forced poses, no staged smiles. Just your truth, beautifully held.
